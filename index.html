Published Paper in the 2023 IEEE International Conference on SOSE

  • For my capstone project during my senior year at Baylor University, I worked with a small team to provide the community with a set of full functional regression testing coverage for two well-established microservice systems.

  • We aimed to demonstrate ways to overcome certain challenges and fine effective strategies when testing microservices. We conducted a case study to identify the best approaches to take to validate a full coverage of tests using service-dependency graph discovery and business process discovery using tracing.

  • I wrote all of the functional regression tests for testing the front-end services of the eShopOnContainers microservice system and worked closely with a team member on the TrainTicket microservice system tests.

  • We reported our findings in our case study paper that was published in the 2023 IEEE International Conference on Service-Oriented System Engineering (SOSE). To view our paper, click here.
